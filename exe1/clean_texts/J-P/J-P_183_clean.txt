Security at Israel ' s Ben - Gurion Airport was reinforced on Thursday evening amid reports of a " serious security incident " near Lod , Israeli media reported , citing sources at the airport ." Somebody told me snipers were present ," Itay Cohen , a photojournalist at the airport , told Walla . " When I got off the bus , I saw two security guards with drawn weapons ." We got rushed into the building and could not see a thing ," Cohen added ." This incident is extremely unusual ." Israel ' s Airports Authority noted that the incident did not interfere with normal airport activity . This is a developing story .
