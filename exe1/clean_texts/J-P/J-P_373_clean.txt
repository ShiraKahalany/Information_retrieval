As an artist , she is very sensitive to and perceptive of the surrounding realities . In her career , Peiper & rsquo ; s works have been on show at numerous solo and group exhibitions in Israel ( in 2023 at the Tel Aviv Bienniale of Crafts and Design at the Eretz Israel Museum ), The United States , and Europe . She works with different techniques , using materials such as gold , silver , stainless steel , aluminum , and copper . When designing sculptures , she combines glass with metal . Her works reflect her personal experience ( as in her solo show & ldquo ; Fish Plus & rdquo ; in 2020 ) and social exposure .
